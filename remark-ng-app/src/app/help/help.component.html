Instructions to use Remark

Creating notes

Remark is an app for a simple storage of notes. You have an opportunity to categorize, arrange them any way you want.
That is why we propose you to create notebooks firstly for easier storage.

You cannot create notes without creating a notebook.

Sharing notebooks

You can share your notes in notebooks with different users using their username.
Click on the notebook you want to share, and then write into the required field a username of a person to share with. Click the button '+ shared'.

Feedback

If you have any unsolved problems or questions to ask us, you can write us in a Feedback. We will response you on e-mail as soon as possible.
Moreover, we are looking forward to your reviews and comments on Remark.
